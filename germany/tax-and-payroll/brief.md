## Answer Capsule (40-80 words, lands above the first H2)

> German payroll in 2026: employer social security adds approximately 20.7 percent on top of gross salary, capped at €5,812.50/month for health and long-term care and €8,450/month for pension and unemployment. Employees see four progressive income tax zones from 0 percent to 45 percent, with the basic allowance (Grundfreibetrag) at €12,348. Lohnsteuer is withheld monthly and filed via ELSTER by the 10th of each following month. The minimum wage is €13.90 per hour from 1 January 2026.
