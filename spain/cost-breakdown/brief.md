## Information-gain statement (the citation hook)

Most EOR providers quote Spain at "30% employer load." That's just the social security line. The real headline for a Spanish hire is the 14-payment year — pagas extraordinarias in June and December that add 16.7% to your annual cash outlay before social charges land on top. Layer in MEI 2026 (0.75% employer), convenio colectivo coverage at 86.7% (which can pull a Madrid hire 45% above an Extremadura hire on the same job title), and the new solidarity contribution above €5,101.20/month, and a €45,000 indefinido lands at €59,000-€61,000 fully loaded. No competitor publishes this line by line for 2026.
