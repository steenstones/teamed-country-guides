## Distinctive angle

Florida is one of the strongest "no state statute + state preemption" frames in the US. It pairs:
- **No state PFL law** (nine states + DC have one; FL doesn't)
- **No state PSL law** (eighteen states + DC have one; FL doesn't)
- **State preemption of cities and counties** via **Florida Statute § 218.077** (enacted 2013, amended 2017) — distinguishing Florida from California / NY / IL where cities can add leave on top.
- **SB 220 (2026 Session)** proposes state-government-employee paid parental leave only; no private-sector impact even if it passes.

The angle: not just "no state statute" (Alabama), not "voluntary insurance carve-out" (Arkansas Act 850) — Florida is "no statute AND blocks cities from filling the gap." Administratively cleanest US state for multi-city leave admin.
