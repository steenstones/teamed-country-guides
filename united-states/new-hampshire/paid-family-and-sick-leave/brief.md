## Page summary

New Hampshire is the cleanest leave compliance environment in the Northeast for private employers. No mandatory state paid family leave, no mandatory paid sick leave. The state runs the **Granite State Paid Family Leave plan (GSPFL)** as a voluntary opt-in, making it the first opt-in (not mandatory) state PFML in the US (launched 1 January 2023). Federal FMLA at 50+ employees provides the statutory floor for unpaid job-protected leave.

The distinctive editorial angle: the GSPFL opt-in decision is a competitive differentiator, not a compliance obligation. Employers who opt in give candidates a state-backed 6-weeks-at-60% benefit via MetLife. Those who don't explain the gap on every hiring call.
