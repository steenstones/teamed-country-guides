## Answer Capsule (40-80 words)

Maine is an at-will state, but the **Earned Paid Leave statute (26 M.R.S. §637)** creates a termination liability most operators miss: accrued unused leave must be paid out at separation for any employer with 10 or more employees. Final pay follows the **next regular payday or within 2 weeks of written demand** under 26 M.R.S. §626. The Maine Human Rights Act (5 M.R.S. §4572) covers employers with 15 or more employees for most claims, with a 300-day EEOC charge window.
