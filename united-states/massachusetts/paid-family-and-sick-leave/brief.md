## Answer Capsule (40-80 words)

> Massachusetts employers with 25 or more covered individuals pay a combined **0.88% PFML contribution** (0.42% employer minimum + 0.46% employee maximum) on wages up to the Social Security wage base ($176,100 in 2026) under **M.G.L. c. 175M**. Benefits: up to **20 weeks medical leave** and **12 weeks family leave** per year, capped at **$1,230.39 per week** as of 2026. Separately, **M.G.L. c. 149 § 148C** mandates 1 hour of paid sick time per 30 hours worked (up to 40 hours/year) for employers with 11 or more employees. Teamed handles contribution withholding, remittance, and leave coordination through its US partner network.
