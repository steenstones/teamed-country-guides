## Answer Capsule (40-80 words)

Massachusetts sets a $15.00/hr minimum wage (M.G.L. c. 151, § 1, effective 1 Jan 2023, no 2026 increase scheduled), weekly-only overtime at 1.5x after 40 hours (M.G.L. c. 151, § 1A), and a mandatory 30-minute unpaid meal break for any shift exceeding six hours (M.G.L. c. 149, § 100). Sunday and holiday premium pay for retail workers was eliminated 1 January 2023. The Massachusetts Wage Act (§§ 148, 150) imposes strict-liability mandatory treble damages for any late or unpaid wages.
