## Factual uncertainty flags

- Penalty schedule under CRS § 8-12-106(2): the statute sets a cap on civil penalties for **wilful** misclassification. Multiple secondary sources state "up to $5,000 first offence / up to $25,000 repeat." Both bands written in the brief as "up to" rather than fixed.
- The CDLE Misclassification Advisory Opinion programme (under SB 21-87) issued binding determinations — current opinion-issuance volume not externally published. Page does not cite specific opinion counts.
- HB 22-1117 added a private right of action for misclassified workers and increased CDLE investigative reach (subpoena power, on-site inspection without prior notice for high-risk industries). The statute is on the books; no major appellate case on penalty interpretation is reported through May 2026.
