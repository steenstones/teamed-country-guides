## Page angles applied

- **Lead angle:** Same-day final pay and the 30-day penalty — the mechanical compliance obligation that catches out-of-state employers.
- **Second angle:** D'Angelo implied-contract risk from handbook language — Nevada-specific doctrine most non-Nevada employers don't know.
- **Third angle:** Hansen public-policy exception with four categories, including workers' comp filing protection.
- **Fourth angle:** NRS 613.330 anti-discrimination and anti-retaliation as the statutory overlay on top of common-law exceptions.
