## Disagreements between sources

- **Standard deduction 2026:** Baldwin CPAs guide cited $3,270 (this is the 2025 figure, likely a drafting error). Official DOR announcement (4 September 2025) confirmed $3,360 for 2026. **Resolution: use $3,360.**
- **UI taxable wage base trajectory:** EY footnote suggests increases may stop at $12,000. Not confirmed by Kentucky OUI. **Resolution: state $12,000 for 2026, flag that future years require annual check.**
- **Historical FUTA credit reduction:** One search result referenced a prior 0.3% credit reduction for Kentucky (from the post-Great Recession loan). This is historical — Kentucky is NOT on the 2026 reduction list. Both IRS and Bloomberg confirm. **Resolution: state 0.6% effective rate, note the historical precedent as buyer-awareness context.**
