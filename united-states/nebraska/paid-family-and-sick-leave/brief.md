## Page summary

Nebraska is a hybrid state for leave compliance in 2026: no state paid family leave programme, but a new state paid sick leave law (NHFWA) that took effect on 1st October 2025 following the passage of Initiative 436 in November 2024. LB 415 (2025) amended the law to introduce employer-size accrual tiers before the effective date.

Federal FMLA applies at 50-plus employees. No state PFML premium applies. No state SDI.

## Critical flag: accrual tier discrepancy

The ROW brief stated accrual tiers as "80+ employees: 56 hrs, 20-79: 40 hrs, <20: 40 hrs unpaid". The Nebraska compliance cache (nebraska.json) and LB 415 references use "20+ employees: 56 hrs, 11-19: 40 hrs, under 11: unpaid". 

**Page uses the compliance cache (20+/11-19) as the verified figure.** The "80+" threshold in the ROW brief appears to be an error. Confirm against NDOL published FAQ at https://dol.nebraska.gov/LaborStandards/PaidSickTime/PSTFAQs before production.
