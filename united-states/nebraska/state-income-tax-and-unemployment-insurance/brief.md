**Prompt correction:** Prompt stated "glide path to 3.99% flat by 2027 — verify current year top rate." Verified: 2026 top rate is **4.55%**, NOT 3.99%. Nebraska is still on the glide path; reaches 3.99% on January 1, 2027.
