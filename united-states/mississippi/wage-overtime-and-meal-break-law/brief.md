## Editorial angle

Mississippi has no state minimum wage, no state overtime statute, and no adult break mandate. Federal FLSA is the entire floor. The page frames this not as a curiosity but as a practical employer decision: a low statutory floor means competitive practice — wages, breaks, overtime norms — is employer-set policy rather than legal obligation. The buyer lens is: "what do I actually have to do, and what should I do to hire well here?"

## Provisional fact flags

- **$684/week FLSA OT salary threshold** — reflects status after judicial rollback of 2024 DOL increases. Status as of 30 May 2026. Confirm before publishing.
- **$7.25/hr federal minimum** — unchanged since 24 July 2009. No federal increase pending as of 30 May 2026 but politically contested.
- **Restaurant exemptions in § 71-1-35** — historically applied to payment timing, not wage rate. Treated as non-material for this page; verify if a restaurant client asks.
